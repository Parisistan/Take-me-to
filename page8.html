Gym

Are you feeling bored at home, or in need of a fun new workout? Why not try following along to one of our suggested videos... From Yoga to HIIT workouts, we've got you covered.

Our Recommendations

Yoga:

If you're for a way to stretch and relax, then look no further than this yoga routine by Brett Larkin. It's aimed at beginners, so people with any level of experience can follow along!

Zumba:

Zumba can be a fun way to get moving and do some exercise! We love this video. It's only 10 minutes long and is also aimed at beginners, so it's a great place to start if you're interested in Zumba!

HIIT (High Intensity Interval Training):

We know that sometimes, exercise isn't the most fun in the world - but what better way to make it fun than to work out to ABBA tunes? This HIIT workout video from emkfit feels more like dancing than exercising, so you can have fun and sing along while you get your body moving!

Recipes

After working out, making sure that you eat a healthy meal is one of the most important things. This website has more than 30 healthy recipes which will make sure you're staying strong!
